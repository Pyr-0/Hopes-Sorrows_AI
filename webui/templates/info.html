Emotional Categories

Hope

Future-oriented positivity, dreams, and aspirations. These blobs float upward with gentle, optimistic movements in warm, golden tones.

Sorrow

Grief, loss, and melancholy. These forms move with slower, more contemplative patterns in deep blues and purples.

Transformative

Growth through adversity, learning from pain. Dynamic, evolving shapes that shift between colors, representing change and growth.

Ambivalent

Mixed emotions, internal conflict. These blobs exhibit contrasting movements and colors, representing emotional complexity.

Reflective

Thoughtful contemplation, philosophical musing. Gentle, meditative movements in neutral, calming tones.
